## What is ZAT?

**ZAT – Zentrum für Assistive Technologien Rhein-Ruhr** (Center for Assistive Technologies Rhine-Ruhr) is a research and transfer hub based at **Hochschule Rhein-Waal**.

We develop, test, and promote **assistive technologies** that help people with disabilities, elderly individuals, and care professionals.

![Team working with a robot](/images/ZAT_team.jpg)

## Our Mission

Technology should empower – not replace – human connection. We bring together:

- **Researchers** from computer science, engineering, and social sciences
- **Care practitioners** from hospitals, nursing homes, and disability services
- **End users** – people who use assistive technologies every day
- **Companies** developing the next generation of smart assistance

## Key Locations

- **Campus Kamp-Lintfort** – Main lab (FabLab & AIS-Lab)
- **Campus Kleve** – Workshops and community events
- **City of Moers** – Innovation campus for digital care

## What We Work On

| Technology | Application |
|------------|-------------|
| Social robots (QTrobot, Pepper) | Education, autism support, elderly care |
| Smart home sensors | Independent living, fall detection |
| Cognitive assistance systems | Memory support, daily structure |
| Easy Language AI tools | Accessibility and inclusion |
