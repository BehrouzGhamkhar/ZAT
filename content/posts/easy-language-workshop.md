## Workshop on March 16, 2026

Together with **Lebenshilfe Kreis Kleve**, ZAT organized a 2.5‑hour workshop about **Easy Language** (Einfache Sprache). Location: Campus Kleve.

![Group workshop with participants](/ZAT/images/posts/easy_language.jpg)


## What We Did

- Explained rules of Easy Language (short sentences, simple words, one idea per sentence)
- Translated a complicated official letter into Easy Language as a group
- Tested an **AI‑based simplification tool** – and discussed its mistakes

## Key Insight

Even experts make texts too complicated. The best feedback came from people who use Easy Language every day.
