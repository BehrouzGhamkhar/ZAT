## March 13, 2026 at Hochschule Niederrhein

Our first **tech4cognition** conference brought together researchers, care professionals, tech companies, and people with disabilities.

![Conference audience and presenter](/zatrobotics/images/posts/tech4cognition.jpg)

## Highlights

- Keynote: "AI that serves – not replaces"
- 8 interactive workshops (robotics, sensors, ethics)
- Exhibition with live demos from 12 research groups
- Matchmaking session between startups and care facilities

## What Participants Said

> "Finally a conference where users are in the room, not just engineers."
