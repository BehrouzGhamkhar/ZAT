## A Special Day at Hochschule Rhein-Waal

On March 18, 2026, we invited **two kindergarten groups** to our labs in Kamp-Lintfort. The goal: bring research closer to children – and learn from them.

![Children watching a robot demonstration](/ZAT/images/posts/kindergarten.jfif)

## What Happened

- QTrobot showed emotions and played a memory game
- Children tried simple **VR glasses** with animal environments
- They drew their own "robot friend" and explained what it should do

## Why This Matters

Young children give honest, unfiltered feedback. They helped us understand:
- Which robot expressions feel friendly
- How to make buttons more intuitive
- What makes a robot "fun"

## Reactions

> "I want the robot to come to my birthday!" – Mia, age 5

We will repeat this format in autumn 2026.
