## About QTrobot

QTrobot is a small expressive humanoid robot designed to help children with autism and other special needs. It combines **evidence-based educational methods** with engaging, predictable interactions.

![QTrobot working with a child](/zatrobotics/images/projects/qtrobot.jfif)

## What We Do at ZAT

- Develop **learning programs** for social skills and emotion recognition
- Integrate QTrobot into kindergarten and school settings
- Train educators and therapists on robot-assisted intervention

## Why It Works

- Predictable, patient, and repeatable
- No social judgment – children feel safe
- Visual and verbal cues combined

## Current Status

We are running pilot studies in **Kamp-Lintfort and Kleve** with local partners. Early feedback shows increased engagement and longer attention spans.
