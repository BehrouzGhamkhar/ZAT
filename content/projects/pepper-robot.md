## Meet Pepper

Pepper is a semi-humanoid robot capable of recognizing faces, emotions, and engaging in natural conversation. It’s used in **care homes, hospitals, and public spaces**.

![Pepper robot talking to a senior](/images/projects/pepper.jfif)

## ZAT Research Areas

- **Cognitive assistance** for people with dementia
- **Daily routine reminders** (medication, appointments)
- **Entertainment and social stimulation** in group settings

## Pilot Project

Together with the **City of Moers**, we are placing Pepper in a senior facility to test:
- Acceptance by residents
- Impact on loneliness
- Practical usefulness for care staff

## Ethical Considerations

We always involve **caregivers, ethicists, and end users** in our design process. Technology should support – not replace – human connection.
